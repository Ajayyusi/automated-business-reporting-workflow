# Business Case

Small and medium service businesses often manage reporting through manual spreadsheets, WhatsApp/Telegram messages, accounting exports, and repeated copy-paste work. This creates delays, inconsistent categories, and limited visibility into cash flow, profit margin, and customer activity.

This project shows how a junior BI/data automation candidate can improve operations with a lightweight reporting workflow:

- Collect income, expenses, customer, and KPI target data.
- Clean and standardize reporting fields.
- Categorize transactions into useful management views.
- Calculate monthly revenue, expenses, net profit, margins, and target performance.
- Export dashboard-ready charts and management report tables.

The goal is practical business improvement, not heavy enterprise architecture.

## Example Business Impact

- Reduce monthly reporting preparation from several hours to a repeatable scripted workflow.
- Improve visibility into revenue, collections, and budget risk.
- Help owners and managers review performance using consistent KPIs.
- Support faster follow-up on unpaid invoices and high-spend expense categories.
